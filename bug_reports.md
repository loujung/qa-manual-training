# Bug Reports — Amazon Website

## BUG-001 — Cart count does not update immediately after adding product

**Environment:**
- Browser: Google Chrome
- OS: Windows
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open Amazon homepage
2. Search for any product
3. Open a product details page
4. Click on "Add to Cart"

**Expected Result:**
- Cart count updates immediately after adding the product

**Actual Result:**
- Cart count updates after a delay or only after page refresh

**Severity:** Minor  
**Priority:** Low
